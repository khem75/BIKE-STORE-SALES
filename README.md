# 🚴 Bike Store Sales Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze bike store sales performance, revenue trends, customer insights, and product category performance. The dashboard helps identify key revenue drivers and business growth opportunities through dynamic visualizations.

## 📊 Key Features
- Total Revenue & Customer KPIs
- Revenue Trend Analysis (Year & Month)
- Revenue by Brand
- Revenue by Product
- Revenue by Store
- Revenue by Category & City
- Detailed Revenue Reports
- Interactive Date and City Filters

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Excel/CSV Dataset
